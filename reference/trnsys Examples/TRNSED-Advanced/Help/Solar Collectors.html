Two modes for selecting the solar collector are available: select from a list or supply the collector parameters.

If the collector is selected from a list, 6 different collectors choices are available:

  • Flat plate Low performance
  • Flat plate Medium performance
  • Flat plate High performance
  • Evacuated tube Low performance
  • Evacuated tube Medium performance
  • Evacuated tube High performance

The figure here below shows efficiency curves for the 6 available collector types

If the collector parameters are to be supplied, it is necessary to supply:

  • The intercept (maximum) efficiency (a0)
  • The negative of the first order loss coefficient (a1)
  • The negative of the second order loss coefficient (a2)
  • The first order incidence angle modifier constant (b0)
  • The second order incidence angle modifier constant (b1)

  Please refer to Type 1 mathematical reference for details on those parameters
